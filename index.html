About the Symposium

Welcome to a symposium inspired by Stranger Things, where ideas flip upside down and creativity takes control. This is your chance to step into the Upside Down—a place where thinking differently is not just allowed, it’s encouraged. From high-energy technical challenges and creative events to fun-filled games and rapid-fire presentations, this symposium is designed to test your skills, speed, teamwork, and imagination. Whether you’re coding, designing, pitching, filming, or competing, every event brings its own twist—because nothing here is ordinary. Just like the Hawkins gang, you’ll need quick thinking, smart collaboration, and a bit of courage to stand out. It’s competitive, it’s exciting, and yes—there’s fun along the way (don’t worry, no Demogorgons… probably). So gear up, bring your A-game, and get ready to experience a symposium where learning meets excitement and creativity meets chaos.

The gate is open. Welcome to the Upside Down.
